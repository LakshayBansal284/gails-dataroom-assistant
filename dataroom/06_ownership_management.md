## Ownership Structure

### Ultimate Beneficial Owner
**Grain Topco Limited** is registered as the Person with Significant Control (PSC) of GAIL'S LIMITED at Companies House.

### Who owns Grain Topco Limited?

| Shareholder | Approximate Stake | Notes |
|------------|-------------------|-------|
| Bain Capital Credit | Majority (~85%) | Boston-based global alternative investment firm, credit and private equity specialist. Led the September 2021 acquisition of Bread Holdings. Reportedly seeking exit via Goldman Sachs-run auction (announced November 2024). |
| EBITDA Investments | Minority | Food ecosystem fund backed by Henry McGovern (founder of AmRest) and Steven K. Winegar. Co-investor alongside Bain Capital since September 2021. |
| Luke Johnson / Risk Capital Partners | ~15% | UK entrepreneur and restaurateur. Invested in Bread Holdings in April 2011. Retained minority stake and board seat following the 2021 Bain Capital acquisition. |
| Management (Tom Molnar et al.) | Small minority (options) | CEO and management team retain equity interests via options. Exact proportion not publicly disclosed. |

### Acquisition History
- **April 2011:** Risk Capital Partners (Luke Johnson) invests in Bread Holdings when GAIL's had approximately £25m in annual sales
- **September 2021:** Bain Capital Credit and EBITDA Investments acquire majority stake in Bread Holdings for approximately £200m enterprise value. Grain Topco Limited incorporated as new holding structure. All prior HSBC bank charges satisfied simultaneously.
- **November 2024:** Sky News reports Goldman Sachs mandated to run a sale process; business valued at up to £500m by analysts. McWin Capital Partners reported in talks to pre-empt (Bloomberg).

## Key Person Risk
Tom Molnar (CEO/co-founder) has been central to the GAIL's brand for over 20 years. His continued involvement is considered a significant factor in the brand's authenticity and product quality positioning. His departure would represent a material risk event for any lender or acquirer.
