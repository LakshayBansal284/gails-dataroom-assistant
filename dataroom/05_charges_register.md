## Key Observations for a Lender

1. **Glas Trust Corporation Limited** is a specialist security trustee firm, commonly used in leveraged finance. Their presence indicates the Grain Topco acquisition debt is held across a syndicate of lenders, not a single bilateral bank. Glas Trust holds security on behalf of the syndicate.

2. **Both outstanding charges are all-assets debentures** — no specific land or IP is identified. This means the charge covers all assets of GAIL'S LIMITED as a going concern, giving the security trustee broad enforcement rights.

3. **A new lender would be structurally subordinated** to Glas Trust unless they negotiate an intercreditor agreement. Any new facility would need to address priority of claims over GAIL'S LIMITED's assets.

4. **The clean sweep of all HSBC charges on 29 October 2021** confirms the Bain Capital acquisition financing fully replaced the prior bank debt on the day of completion.

5. **The quantum of Grain Topco's acquisition debt is not disclosed** in publicly available filings. A lender would need to request this via the information undertakings in the existing facility agreement.
