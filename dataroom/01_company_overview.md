## Business Description
GAIL's Bakery is a premium artisan bakery and café chain. It was founded in Hampstead, London in 2005 as a retail offshoot of The Bread Factory, a wholesale bakery established in Hendon in the early 1990s by Yael (Gail) Mejia. The business sells sourdough bread, pastries, coffee and café-style food, positioned at the premium end of the high-street bakery market.

As of March 2025, GAIL's operated 170 UK locations. By February 2025 (end of FY2025) the estate had grown to 185 sites. The business is targeting approximately 40 further new openings in FY2026, which would take the estate to approximately 225 sites.

## Corporate Structure
GAIL'S LIMITED is an audit-exempt subsidiary. The group structure is:
- **Grain Topco Limited** — ultimate parent; files consolidated group accounts
- **Bread Holdings Limited** — intermediate holding company
- **GAIL'S LIMITED (06055393)** — retail operating entity (this filing entity)
- **The Bread Factory** — wholesale manufacturing subsidiary (sibling entity)
- **Bertinet Bakery** — wholesale subsidiary (sibling entity)

Because GAIL'S LIMITED files as an audit-exempt subsidiary, the most complete financial picture comes from Grain Topco's consolidated accounts, which are filed alongside the subsidiary accounts at Companies House.
