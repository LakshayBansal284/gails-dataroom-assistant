# Grain Topco — Financial Results FY2024
**Source: Grain Topco Limited consolidated accounts, year to 29 February 2024 (filed 25 November 2024 at Companies House); British Baker (December 2024); The Grocer (December 2024)**

## Headline Figures — FY2024 (Year to 29 February 2024)

| Metric | FY2024 | FY2023 | Change |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| GAIL's retail revenue | £179.1m | £135.3m | +32% |
| Wholesale revenue | £83.7m | £46.5m | +80% |
| Group total revenue | £231.8m | £181.8m | +28% |
| Group adjusted EBITDA | £43.0m | ~£30.7m | +40% |
| EBITDA margin | ~18.6% | ~16.9% | +170bps |
| Pre-tax loss (group) | £7.4m loss | £15.4m loss | Improved |
| Gross profit margin | ~39% | ~39% | Flat (slight dip) |
| New GAIL's sites opened | 21 | 28 | — |
| Total GAIL's sites (Feb 2024) | ~153 | ~116 | +37 |

## Key Points
- GAIL's retail revenue grew 32% — fastest growing and most profitable part of Grain Topco group
- Group total revenue grew 28% to £231.8m
- Group EBITDA grew 40% to £43m — significant operating leverage
- Pre-tax loss narrowed substantially from £15.4m to £7.4m — improving trajectory
- Gross profit margin held approximately flat at 39%, with slight dip noted due to staff cost and utility increases
- 21 new GAIL's sites opened, including first openings outside south east England (Manchester)
- Group had approximately 3,500 employees
- The Bread Factory wholesale revenue grew nearly a fifth to £83.7m
- GAIL's estate reached approximately 153 sites

## Strategic Notes
- Business opened at Waitrose — branded Gail's fixtures in 64 Waitrose stores (extended partnership)
- Production at Milton Keynes site continuing to grow; new production lines added
- Tom Martin (Britain's Best Loaf 2024 winner) appointed as head baker at The Bread Factory Manchester
- New purpose-built Manchester facility planned within two years
- By end of FY2024, valuation expected at approximately £500m (analyst estimates ahead of sale process)

## Comparison to Pre-Acquisition
- FY2021 (last year before Bain Capital): Bread Holdings revenue £57.1m
- FY2024 GAIL's retail alone: £179.1m — more than triple the pre-acquisition baseline
