# Grain Topco — Financial Results FY2025
**Source: Grain Topco Limited consolidated accounts, year to 28 February 2025 (filed 27 November 2025 at Companies House); British Baker (December 2025); The Grocer (December 2025)**

## Headline Figures — FY2025 (Year to 28 February 2025)

| Metric | FY2025 | FY2024 | Change |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| GAIL's retail revenue | £219.8m | £179.1m | +23% |
| Group total revenue | £278m | £231.8m | +20% |
| Group adjusted EBITDA | £53.6m | £43.0m | +19.7% |
| Pre-tax loss (group) | £7.8m loss | £7.4m loss | Widened |
| Gross profit margin | 38.2% | 38.8% | -60bps |
| Operating margin | 9.2% | 9.5% | -30bps |
| New GAIL's sites opened | 36 | 21 | — |
| Total GAIL's sites (Feb 2025) | 185 | ~153 | +32 |
| Employees (group, Feb 2025) | ~4,000+ | ~3,500 | +~500 |
| Store pre-opening costs | £5.1m | — | — |

## Revenue Breakdown FY2025
- GAIL's retail revenue: £219.8m (79% of group total)
- Wholesale revenue (The Bread Factory + Bertinet): approximately £58m
- Total group revenue: £278m

## Key Points
- Revenue grew 23% for GAIL's retail and 20% at group level
- Adjusted group EBITDA grew 19.7% to £53.6m — absolute EBITDA growth despite margin compression
- Pre-tax loss widened slightly from £7.4m to £7.8m, driven by £5.1m store pre-opening costs and rising staff/energy costs
- Gross profit margin declined from 38.8% to 38.2% due to staff cost inflation and utility costs
- Operating margin declined from 9.5% to 9.2%
- 36 new GAIL's sites opened in FY2025 including first openings in south west England
- Total estate reached 185 sites by end of FY2025
- Headcount grew by approximately 500 to over 4,000 employees
- Group targeting approximately 40 new openings in FY2026
- Sales circa 20% higher for the period February 2024 to February 2025

## Management Comment
"We are pleased to have delivered strong year-on-year growth. This performance is underpinned by the increasing demand for high-quality, nutrient dense food, and by the support of the communities we serve." — GAIL's spokesperson

## Context
The FY2025 results were filed while a Goldman Sachs-run sale process was underway. The business was named Bakery Retailer of the Year 2025 at the Baking Industry Awards.
